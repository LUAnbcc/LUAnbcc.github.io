Services

Radio provision - We can provide a range of radios for your event, including mobile, handheld and fixed.

End to end encryption - All our radios come with end to end encryption as standard, with a dedicated private key for each client to ensure data security.

Audio and GPS recording - Both audio and GPS data is securely stored on-site, and immediately accessible in case of incidents.

Emergency button - Each radio has a red 'emergency button', which notifies a manager or the control room of a serious incident.

Why choose us?

Run by a HCPC registered Paramedic, we understand the unique needs of medical teams at events, and how critical communication is key.

Experts in our field, there is no project too big or too small. From small responder teams through to multi-agency operations, we are the perfect partner for your event.

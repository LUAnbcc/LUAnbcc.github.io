Services

- Radio hire

We can provide pre-programmed radios, repeaters and accessories to intergrate with your existing network, or as a standalone system. These include mobile, handheld and fixed radios.

- Consultancy

Our team of experts can provide advice on the best solution for your event, whether it is a small team, a large event or a multi-agency operation.

- Turnkey solutions

We can provide a complete turnkey solution for your event from start to finish, as well as provide long-term rental and support.



What we offer

Network - Our network of radios is secure, reliable and easy to manage.

End to end encryption - All our radios come with end to end encryption as standard, with a dedicated private key for each client to ensure data security.

Audio and GPS recording - Both audio and GPS data is securely stored on-site, and immediately accessible in case of incidents.

Emergency button - Each radio has a red 'emergency button', which notifies a manager or the control room of a serious incident.
